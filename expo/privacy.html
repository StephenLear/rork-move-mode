Privacy Policy for Move Mode

Last Updated: February 2, 2026

Overview

Move Mode ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Don't Collect

Move Mode does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. We do not:

  • Collect your name, email, or contact information
  • Track your location
  • Access your camera or photos
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Share any data with third parties
  • Display advertisements

Local Data Storage

All data generated by Move Mode is stored locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Your daily check-ins (energy, body, mind states)
  • Session history
  • App preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Data Deletion

To delete all your Move Mode data, simply delete the app from your device. All locally stored data will be permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

Move Mode does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of Move Mode after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Third-Party Services

Move Mode does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Your Rights

Since Move Mode does not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete from our systems. All your data exists only on your device.
