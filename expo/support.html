Move Mode Support

Getting Started

Move Mode is designed to be simple and intuitive. Here's how to use it:

  1. Open the app - You'll immediately see the daily check-in screen
  2. Select your state - Tap to choose your current energy level, body feeling, and mental state
  3. Generate your session - Tap "Generate Today's Move"
  4. Review your session - See the movements chosen for you
  5. Start moving - Tap "Start Moving" and follow the guided session
  6. Complete at your pace - Use play, pause, and skip as needed

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any equipment?

No equipment is required. All movements can be performed with just your body. You may want a yoga mat or comfortable surface for floor exercises, but it's not necessary.

How long are the sessions?

Sessions typically range from 5-20 minutes, depending on the movements selected for your current state.

Can I skip movements?

Yes! Use the skip button to move to the next exercise if you need to. There's no pressure to complete every movement.

What do Reset, Flow, and Build mean?

  • Reset: Gentle mobility and breathing exercises to restore and recenter
  • Flow: Balanced movement combining gentle strength and mobility
  • Build: Light strength and conditioning movements

Can I do the same session multiple times?

While Move Mode generates a session based on how you feel each day, you can always return to movements that work well for you.

Is Move Mode suitable for beginners?

Yes! All movements are beginner-friendly and include modifications. Always listen to your body and stop if anything feels uncomfortable.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. Once downloaded, Move Mode works completely offline. All sessions and guidance are included in the app.

Does Move Mode track my data?

No. Move Mode stores your check-ins and session history locally on your device only. We don't collect, transmit, or sell any data.

Can I use Move Mode every day?

Absolutely! Move Mode is designed for daily use. However, there's no pressure - use it whenever you feel like moving.

Technical Support

The app isn't working properly

Try these steps:

  1. Close the app completely and reopen it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check for app updates in the App Store
  4. If problems persist, contact support (details below)

I found a bug

We appreciate bug reports! Please email us with:

  • Description of the issue
  • Steps to reproduce it
  • Your device model and iOS version
